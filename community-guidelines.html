Community Guidelines

Our shared values for maintaining a supportive and respectful environment

Our Community Values

Mind Sync is built on the belief that everyone deserves a safe space to discuss mental health. Our community thrives when members feel respected, supported, and heard. These guidelines outline how we can work together to create and maintain this environment.

By participating in our community, you agree to uphold these values and follow these guidelines in all interactions.

Be Supportive

Our community is first and foremost a space for support:

  • Approach conversations with empathy and understanding
  • Acknowledge that everyone's mental health journey is unique
  • Offer encouragement and constructive support when appropriate
  • Listen to others' experiences without judgment
  • Avoid dismissing or minimizing others' feelings or experiences

Respect Privacy

Maintaining privacy and confidentiality is essential:

  • Do not share personal information of other members without explicit permission
  • Consider carefully what personal information you choose to share
  • What's shared in the community should stay in the community
  • Do not take screenshots or share conversations outside the platform
  • Respect others' boundaries regarding personal questions or topics

Communicate Respectfully

Respectful communication is the foundation of our community:

  • Use inclusive and non-discriminatory language
  • Disagree respectfully without personal attacks
  • Avoid inflammatory or provocative language
  • Be mindful of cultural differences and varied perspectives
  • Give others the benefit of the doubt—assume good intentions
  • Consider the impact of your words, not just your intentions

Content Restrictions

To maintain a safe environment, the following content is not permitted:

  • Detailed descriptions of self-harm methods or suicide plans
  • Graphic descriptions of trauma that may trigger others
  • Content that glorifies or encourages harmful behaviors
  • Hate speech, discrimination, or content targeting any group
  • Spam, advertisements, or promotional content
  • Misinformation about mental health conditions or treatments

If you see content that violates these guidelines, please report it to our moderators.

Medical Advice Disclaimer

Mind Sync is a peer support community, not a source of professional medical advice:

  • Do not provide specific medical advice as if you are a professional
  • Share personal experiences, but clarify they are just that—personal
  • Encourage seeking professional help for serious concerns
  • Never advise others to stop prescribed treatments or medications
  • Be cautious when sharing resources—prioritize reputable sources

Moderation Approach

Our moderation team works to ensure these guidelines are followed:

  • Content that violates our guidelines may be removed
  • Members who repeatedly violate guidelines may receive warnings
  • Severe or continued violations may result in temporary or permanent account restrictions
  • Moderation decisions can be appealed by contacting our support team
  • We strive to be fair, consistent, and transparent in our moderation

Crisis Support

If you or someone else is in immediate danger or crisis:

  • Contact emergency services in your area immediately
  • Use crisis helplines: call +91-9492935222 (9AM-5PM)
  • Our platform is not equipped to provide immediate crisis intervention
  • Moderators may reach out to provide resources if they observe concerning content
